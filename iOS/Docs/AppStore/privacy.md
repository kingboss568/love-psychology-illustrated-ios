# 戀愛心理全圖解 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-28

戀愛心理全圖解 is an educational visual-guide app. The app works with bundled local content and does not require an account.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library access.

## Purchases

The Pro unlock is handled by Apple App Store In-App Purchase. Apple processes purchase and payment information under Apple's policies. The app only checks StoreKit entitlement state to unlock local content.
